Main Characters

The main Characters in One Piece are the StrawHat Pirates, their captain is Luffy and there are currently 10 People on this crew.

The 10 StrawHat pirates names are Luffy, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Nico Robin, Franky, Brooke and Jinbei.
